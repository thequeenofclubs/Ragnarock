# Materials and Durability

## Overview
Ragnarock is constructed from materials chosen for mechanical stability, acoustic neutrality, and long-term reliability. The enclosure, drivers, and internal components are designed to withstand high output levels and extended use.

---

# Enclosure Materials

## Cabinet
- Material: Aspen hardwood, 5-layer laminated
- Internal volume: 9.5 L
- Bracing: Single-axis 1×1 in crossbrace
- Damping: Fiberglass on four interior faces
- Total mass: 20.7 lb

Aspen hardwood provides a rigid, low-resonance structure. The laminated construction increases stiffness and reduces panel vibration.

## Finish
The exterior finish is selected for durability and resistance to wear. The hardwood shell provides natural damping and structural integrity.

---

# Driver Materials

## Low-Frequency Driver
- Cone: Paper
- Surround: Rubber
- Basket: Stamped steel
- Voice coil: Kapton former, 4-layer winding
- Magnet: Ferrite

These materials provide a balance of stiffness, damping, and thermal stability.

## Mid/High Drivers
- Cone: Paper
- Surround: Rubber
- Basket: Brass
- Voice coil: 0.5 in
- Magnet: Ferrite

The brass basket offers rigidity and resistance to deformation under thermal cycling.

## Passive Radiator
- Cone: Carbon fiber
- Surround: Rubber
- Basket: Cast aluminum

The carbon fiber diaphragm maintains structural integrity at high displacement.

---

# Electronics and Amplifier

## Amplifier Module
- Class D topology
- Mounted to a thermally conductive structure
- Designed for stable operation under continuous load

## DSP Hardware
- Raspberry Pi with HiFiBerry DAC/ADC
- 24-bit, 384 kHz processing
- 102-band total processing capability

# Environmental Durability

Ragnarock is designed to tolerate:
- Moderate humidity
- Temperature variation
- Mechanical vibration
- Transport-related shock

The hardwood enclosure and robust driver materials provide long-term stability.

---

# Durability Testing

The system is validated through:
- Long-duration high-SPL stress testing
- Thermal cycling
- Mechanical shock and vibration testing
- Continuous playback endurance tests

These tests ensure consistent performance and reliability over time.
