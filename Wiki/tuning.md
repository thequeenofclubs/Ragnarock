# Frequency Response and Tuning

## Overview
Ragnarock is tuned for a balanced, dynamic sound signature that maintains clarity at all playback levels. The tuning reflects the combined behavior of the Tang Band W6-1139SIF, the Samsung OEM mid/high drivers, and the Dayton Epique passive radiator, all managed by a linear-phase dynamic DSP crossover.

---

# Low-Frequency Tuning

## Alignment
- Enclosure volume: 9.5 L
- Passive radiator tuning: 31 Hz (measured)
- LF driver: Tang Band W6-1139SIF with 12 mm Xmax

The alignment provides strong low-frequency extension relative to enclosure size. The passive radiator handles the majority of displacement below 40 Hz, reducing distortion and mechanical stress on the LF driver.

## Behavior
- Full response: 1 to 26.7 kHz
- Linear response: 29 to 23.8 kHz
- Peak output: 124 dBZ
- 1 m output: 112 dBA
- 4 m output: 92 dBA

The system maintains low distortion and stable tuning even at high playback levels.

---

# Midrange Tuning

## Drivers
- Dual 2-inch Samsung OEM drivers
- Paper cone, brass basket, 0.5-inch voice coil
- Approximately 2 mm Xmax

The midrange is voiced for clarity and natural timbre. The drivers operate above the dynamic crossover point and are protected by a 38-band compressor that prevents over-excursion.

## Behavior
The midrange maintains consistent tonality across a wide listening angle. The brass basket provides rigidity that reduces mechanical coloration.

---

# High-Frequency Tuning

The upper range is shaped to provide detail without harshness. The paper cones of the 2-inch drivers exhibit smooth breakup behavior, which contributes to a natural high-frequency roll-off.

---

# DSP Processing

## Crossover
- Linear-phase dynamic crossover
- Frequency-dependent gain staging
- Phase-coherent summation between LF and MF/HF drivers

## Compression
- 64-band isophonic compressor for tonal consistency
- 38-band protection compressor for driver safety

## Latency
- Analog input: 11 ms
- Bluetooth: 112 ms
- AirPlay: 2011 ms

---

# Design Intent
The tuning aims to deliver:
- Strong but controlled bass
- Clear and natural midrange
- Smooth high-frequency response
- Consistent tonality across playback levels
- Stable behavior in both nearfield and room-filling scenarios
